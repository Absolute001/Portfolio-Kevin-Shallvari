Kevin Shallvari

Born in Tirana (AL) on June 12th 1994, I'm a young guy with a passion for music and all things geeky!
I have been making music for 12 years with the alias Leonardo and I now want to make the next step: INTRODUCE MYSELF TO THE WORLD
I've spent the first half of my life in Southern Italy, in the gorgeous region of Puglia, before moving up North to Piemonte, where I've lived ever since.
It's here, amongst the hills and vines of the Langhe, that I've developed my love for music: I started collecting RECORDS before discovering the production side of music, especially techno, and starting to make my own. However the little town where I live feels like a cage to me, so I decided to create this webpage to fully express myself !

SPIN THAT...

When deciding on what records to play as a dj, I love to focus on what that particular record might be able to spark in the body and the soul of whoever is listening, could that be an emotion or a not-so-hidden message laying underneath the beat, while also setting the mood for the night. While stuck in quarantine due to Covid-19, I've been asked by "If It's Good I'll Play It" — a collective of dj and producers — to create a dj set that could help people be more positive and filled with good vibes.

SKILLS

I don't simply select records, I can also use many software for audio production.
They all needed to compose my "Vertigo Venus" EP.
