LEONARDO

Born in Tirana (AL) on June 12th 1994, I'm a young guy with a passion for music and all things geeky!
I have been making music for 12 years with the alias Leonardo and I now want to make the next step: INTRODUCE MYSELF TO THE WORLD

OUT OF THE CAGE

I've spent the first half of my life in Southern Italy, in the gorgeous region of Puglia, before moving up North to Piemonte, where I've lived ever since.
It's here, amongst the hills and vines of the Langhe, that I've developed my love for music: I started collecting RECORDS before discovering the production side of music, especially techno, and starting to make my own.
